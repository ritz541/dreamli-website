Child Safety Standards & CSAE Policy

Last Updated: December 4, 2025

App Name: Dreamli
Developer: Ritesh Chavan

Zero-Tolerance Policy

Dreamli has a strict zero-tolerance policy against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE). We are committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users and strictly prohibit any content or behavior that endangers children.

Prohibited Content

The following is strictly prohibited on Dreamli:

  • Any depiction of sexual violence or non-consensual sexual content involving minors
  • Grooming, solicitation, or any attempt to engage minors in sexual conversations or acts
  • Sharing, storing, or promoting Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)
  • Distribution of any material depicting the sexual exploitation of children
  • Facilitating contact between adults and minors for the purpose of sexual exploitation
  • Production, distribution, or possession of child sexual abuse material in any form
⚠️ Warning: Violations of this policy will result in immediate account termination, legal action, and reporting to law enforcement authorities.

Reporting and Enforcement

We employ the following mechanisms to ensure safety:

In-App Reporting

Users can report suspicious content or behavior directly within the app via the "Report" function found on every post and user profile. Reports are reviewed by our safety team within 24 hours.

Content Removal

Any content identified as CSAM or CSAE will be removed immediately upon detection. We use a combination of automated detection tools and manual review by trained moderators.

Account Termination

Any user found to be engaging in the distribution or promotion of CSAE material will have their account permanently terminated. We maintain records of terminated accounts and share information with relevant authorities when appropriate.

Technical Safeguards

We implement the following technical measures:

  • Automated content filtering and detection systems
  • Age verification mechanisms for new accounts
  • User behavior monitoring to detect suspicious patterns
  • Regular security audits and updates

Cooperation with Law Enforcement

In accordance with US law (18 U.S.C. § 2258A) and international safety standards, we will report any confirmed instances of CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant local law enforcement authorities. We maintain detailed records of all reports and cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations.

Age Requirements

Dreamli is intended for users 13 years of age and older. We verify age during the registration process and take steps to prevent access by underage users. Users who provide false age information will have their accounts terminated immediately.

Parental Guidance

We encourage parents and guardians to:

  • Monitor their children's online activity and app usage
  • Educate children about online safety and the dangers of CSAE
  • Establish open communication about any suspicious interactions
  • Report any concerning behavior to us immediately
